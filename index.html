1. Where We Are Today

Current SWOT Analysis

Click any quadrant for details

Strengths

  • Skilled workforce with high technical competence
  • Team cohesiveness & effective communication
  • ISO 27001 process improvement underway
  • Flexible, adaptable small team

Weaknesses

  • Missing key roles (PMO, BA, Cyber Lead)
  • Limited staff causes "double-hatting"
  • No certifications or growth plans
  • No documented IT governance framework

Opportunities

  • Upskilling through formalized training
  • Build governance framework from scratch
  • Automation tools to reduce manual work
  • IT as business enabler (cloud, AI)

Threats

  • Rapid technological changes (AI, cloud)
  • Evolving cybersecurity threats
  • Siloed operations across entities
  • Difficulty adopting new tools
