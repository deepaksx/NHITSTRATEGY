3. How We Get There

RISE as Technology Hub

Simple Cost-Plus Model for Centralized Technology Procurement

The Model

Cost-Plus Model

Entity pays = Vendor Cost + X% Margin

  • M1 RISE becomes NH's central technology buyer
  • M2 Full transparency: Entity sees vendor quote + margin
  • M3 Net cost offset by better pricing + reduced overhead
How It Works

Simple 4-Step Process

Streamlined Procurement Flow

  • S1 Entity needs technology → Emails RISE
  • S2 RISE gets vendor quotes → Shows entity
  • S3 Entity approves → Issues PO to RISE
  • S4 RISE orders & delivers → Entity pays RISE
The Rules

Operating Rules

Clear Boundaries for All Parties

  • R1 RISE-approved items: Exclusive channel
  • R2 Declined items: Entity buys independently
  • R3 SLA bypass: Entity can go direct if RISE fails
SLAs

Service Level Agreements

Response Times & Performance Targets

  • L1 Critical: 2 hours | Urgent: 4 hours | Normal: 8 hours
  • L2 95% SLA compliance, 98% quote accuracy
  • L3 3 months below 90% = Automatic termination
Governance

Governance & Exit Strategy

Controls, Audits & Protection

  • G1 Monthly NH IT + RISE review, Quarterly steering
  • G2 Random audits: 10 transactions/month + quarterly full
  • G3 Exit anytime: 90-day notice, zero penalties
